Notes :

This app for solving Systems of Linear Equations 
(AX=B) using Gauss Jordan method.
 
-Sourcecode available in Lazarus

-build in Lazarus 3.2

-Download folder : GaussJordan on https://github.com/nix97/Matrix-Matrices 

-Feel free to use & modify.


Tips:

-To smalling the exe file size, can use strip *.exe (example: strip proGaussJordan.exe) at console.
-When rebuild project, the *.exe size approx 55 MB, after "strip" become approx 6 MB.
